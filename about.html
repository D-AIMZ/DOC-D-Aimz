Who We Are

MedicalAssist is Uganda's premier digital healthcare platform, connecting patients with quality medical services — from doctor consultations and pharmacy deliveries to laboratory diagnostics.

Founded in 2024, we've grown to serve thousands of Ugandans across all regions, making healthcare accessible, affordable, and convenient through technology.

Our network includes licensed doctors, accredited pharmacies, and certified laboratories — all vetted to ensure you receive the highest standard of care.
